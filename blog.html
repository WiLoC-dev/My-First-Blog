MY EXPERIENCE

My Name is Akachukwu Godswill Nzube, a four (400) level Computer Engineering student of Micheal Okpara University Of Agriculture, Umudike, Abia State (MOUAU). I want to start by giving a brief write up about SIWES;
The student Industrial Work Experience Scheme is a program designed for students in Nigerian university and polytechnics to gain practical work, experience in their chosen field, usually lasts for a period of 3-6 months. During this time , students are placed in industries related to thier field of study to work and gain hands-on experience.
I choose Naprima Ideal Technology Limited for my SIWES; because they offer a unique opportunity to gain practical experience in the teh industry. They provide hands-on training in programming languages and other tech relatd skills which is essential for me to gain practical knowledge.
